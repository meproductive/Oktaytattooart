About Me

I am a tattoo artist with 15 years of experience, skilled in all styles, focused on customer satisfaction, and working in a completely sterile environment with a professional approach. I started my craft in Turkiye, provided academic training, and helped nurture new artists. Now, as a 4-time award-winning tattoo artist, I have moved to the United States to showcase my art to the world.
